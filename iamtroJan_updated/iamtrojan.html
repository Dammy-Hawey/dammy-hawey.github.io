Cybersecurity Tips

Beginner

  • Use strong and unique passwords.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Avoid clicking suspicious links in emails.

Intermediate

  • Understand phishing and social engineering tactics.
  • Regularly update software and devices.
  • Use antivirus and firewall protections.

Advanced

  • Learn how to identify and mitigate zero-day vulnerabilities.
  • Practice ethical hacking skills responsibly.
  • Monitor network traffic and logs.
